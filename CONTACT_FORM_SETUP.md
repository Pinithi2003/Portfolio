# Contact Form Setup Instructions

Your contact form is now configured with **Web3Forms** - a free service that will send form submissions directly to your email!

## Steps to Activate:

### 1. Get Your Free Access Key
1. Go to **https://web3forms.com**
2. Enter your email: **pransiluni@gmail.com**
3. Click "Create Access Key"
4. Copy the access key you receive

### 2. Add Your Access Key
1. Open `app/page.tsx`
2. Find line with: `value="YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE"`
3. Replace `YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE` with your actual access key
4. Save the file

### 3. That's It!
Your contact form is now live! When someone submits the form:
- You'll receive an email at **pransiluni@gmail.com**
- The visitor will see a success message
- The form will automatically reset

## Features Included:
✅ Email notifications to your inbox
✅ Success/error messages
✅ Form validation
✅ Loading state while sending
✅ Auto-reset after successful submission
✅ Unlimited submissions (free)

## Testing:
1. Fill out the form on your website
2. Click "Send Message"
3. You should receive an email within seconds

**Note:** Make sure to replace the access key before deploying your portfolio!
